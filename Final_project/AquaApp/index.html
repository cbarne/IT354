Dive in

How to get interested

  • Visit Local Aquatic store to window shop a type of fish / invertebrates.
  • Gather inspiration from nature / the web.
  • Study up on tank sizes, nitrate cycle, materials that are tank 'Ok' and the type of filter needed.
  • Begin gathering supplies to start the tank.

Educate One-self

This hobby is not for those unwilling to take the time to learn.
Learning is vital to staying healthy and keeping the mind at peace.
As one embarks on their journey into aquascaping, it can become a struggle, a toil, a test of mental will power at times. Failure is to be expected, but dont lose hope. Failure is the key to success. Through failure one learns the true lessons.
As plants grow, fish thrive; algae grows, fish die. It is a cycle of nature.
